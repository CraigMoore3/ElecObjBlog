## Serial Communication

* **Asynchronous** - one-way. Signal is sent, recieved. The primary type we'll be working with. **(RS-232)**
* **Synchronous** - Hardware is synced, constant back-and-forth communication. **(i^2C, SPI, I-Wire)**
